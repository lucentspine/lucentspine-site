Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Information Collection

Lucent Spine, PLLC collects information that you provide when contacting us through our website, including name, email address, phone number, and case details.

Use of Information

Information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing expert medical consultation services and responding to inquiries. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required by law.

Confidentiality

All communications and medical information are handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with HIPAA regulations and professional medical ethics standards.

Submitting information through this website does not create an attorney-client or physician-patient relationship. A formal engagement agreement is required before Dr. Lee can serve as an expert in your case.
