Independent Medical Examinations (IME)

Dr. Lee conducts thorough, objective independent medical examinations for complex spine surgery cases. Each IME includes:

  • Comprehensive review of medical records and imaging studies
  • Detailed patient history and interview
  • Complete physical examination of the spine and neurological system
  • Analysis of treatment appropriateness and adherence to standard of care
  • Causation determination (relationship between injury and claimed condition)
  • Prognosis and future care needs
  • Disability and impairment rating (when applicable)

Written Report Includes:

  • Summary of medical history and prior treatment
  • Findings from physical examination
  • Review and interpretation of diagnostic imaging
  • Medical opinions on causation, diagnosis, and prognosis
  • Analysis of treatment appropriateness
  • Recommendations for future care (if applicable)
  • Impairment ratings per AMA Guidelines (if requested)

Dr. Lee's IME reports are thorough, well-documented, and supported by medical literature. His active surgical practice ensures that his evaluations reflect real-world clinical standards.

Medical Record Review & Analysis

For cases that do not require a physical examination, Dr. Lee provides comprehensive medical record review and analysis. This service is ideal for:

  • Pre-litigation screening and case merit evaluation
  • Standard of care analysis in medical malpractice cases
  • Treatment appropriateness review
  • Causation analysis in personal injury cases
  • Disability and impairment assessment

Analysis Includes:

  • Detailed review of complete medical records
  • Interpretation of diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT, X-rays)
  • Evaluation of surgical indications and techniques
  • Identification of deviations from standard of care
  • Analysis of surgical complications and their causes
  • Assessment of alternative treatment options
  • Literature support for medical opinions

Record review is often the most cost-effective initial step in evaluating a potential case.
