About Dr. Stanley S. Lee

Board-Certified Orthopaedic Spine Surgeon

Dr. Stanley S. Lee is a board-certified orthopaedic surgeon specializing in adult and pediatric spine surgery. He brings more than 20 years of clinical experience to medical-legal consultation, providing objective, evidence-based analysis for attorneys representing both plaintiff and defense clients.

Dr. Lee maintains an active surgical practice while serving as an expert consultant—a combination that ensures his medical opinions reflect current standards of care and real-world clinical experience, not merely theoretical knowledge.

University of Michigan trained for both undergraduate and medical education, Dr. Lee completed his surgical training at Loyola University Medical Center and an elite fellowship in adult and pediatric spine surgery at Washington University/Barnes Jewish Hospital in St. Louis.

Professional Background

Education & Training

Dr. Lee's educational journey began at the University of Michigan, where he completed both his undergraduate degree (BS in Biomedical Sciences through the prestigious Inteflex Program) and his medical degree. This dual education at one of the nation's premier medical institutions provided a strong foundation in both basic science and clinical medicine.

He completed his general surgery internship and orthopaedic surgery residency at Loyola University Medical Center in Chicago, gaining comprehensive surgical training and developing expertise in musculoskeletal care.

Dr. Lee then pursued fellowship training in adult and pediatric spine surgery at Barnes Jewish Hospital/Washington University in St. Louis—one of the most prestigious spine surgery programs in the country. This dual fellowship training is rare and provides Dr. Lee with expertise spanning the entire age spectrum, from pediatric congenital deformities to adult degenerative conditions.

Professional Experience

2004-2008: Following fellowship, Dr. Lee joined the faculty at the University of Michigan Medical School as a Clinical Lecturer in Orthopaedic Spine Surgery, teaching medical students and residents while maintaining a busy clinical practice.

2008-2012: Dr. Lee served as an employed orthopaedic spine surgeon at Allegiance Health in Jackson, Michigan, building a comprehensive spine surgery practice and establishing relationships throughout southeast Michigan.

2011-Present: Founded Lucent Spine, PLLC, a medical expert consulting practice focused on providing high-quality, evidence-based medical-legal consultation to attorneys.

2012-Present: Established private practice with Specialists in Spine Surgery, maintaining active surgical privileges at 9 hospitals across the greater Detroit and Ann Arbor metropolitan areas.

Professional Philosophy

Evidence-Based Medicine

Dr. Lee's approach to medical-legal consultation is grounded in evidence-based medicine. Every opinion is supported by peer-reviewed medical literature, established clinical practice guidelines, and the standards set forth by professional societies such as the North American Spine Society.

Objectivity & Integrity

True expert consultation requires objectivity. Dr. Lee provides the same thorough, honest analysis whether working with plaintiff or defense counsel. His goal is to help attorneys and juries understand the medical facts of a case, not to advocate for a predetermined outcome.

Active Clinical Practice

Dr. Lee believes that credible expert testimony must be rooted in ongoing clinical experience. By maintaining an active surgical practice across 9 hospitals, he ensures that his knowledge reflects current medical standards, surgical techniques, and the real-world challenges that physicians face in caring for patients with spine conditions.

Clear Communication

Complex medical concepts must be explained clearly to judges, juries, and attorneys who lack medical training. Dr. Lee emphasizes clear, jargon-free communication that makes spine surgery understandable to non-medical audiences while maintaining technical accuracy.

Current Practice

Lucent Spine, PLLC

Founded in 2011, Lucent Spine, PLLC is Dr. Lee's medical expert consulting practice. The practice provides:

  • Expert witness testimony for depositions and trial
  • Independent medical examinations (IME)
  • Medical record review and analysis
  • Case consultation and strategy
  • Second opinion evaluations

The practice name reflects Dr. Lee's commitment to clarity ("lucent" meaning clear or translucent)—bringing clarity to complex medical-legal questions.

Unique Qualifications

Dual Training: Adult & Pediatric

Dr. Lee completed fellowship training in both adult and pediatric spine surgery—a rare combination. This dual expertise allows him to evaluate cases involving patients of any age, from congenital pediatric deformities to adult degenerative conditions.

University of Michigan Connection

Educated at University of Michigan for both undergraduate (BS Biomedical Sciences) and medical school (MD), Dr. Lee also served as Clinical Lecturer in Orthopaedic Spine Surgery (2004-2008). This deep connection to a premier academic medical center reinforces his commitment to evidence-based practice and teaching.

Bilingual Capabilities

Dr. Lee speaks conversational Cantonese and Mandarin, allowing him to communicate with diverse patient populations and review cases involving non-English speaking patients. This linguistic capability is valuable in evaluating cultural and communication factors in medical care.

Multi-State Licensure

Licensed to practice medicine in Michigan (since 2004) and Louisiana (since 2025), Dr. Lee can provide expert consultation across state lines and understands varying state medical regulations and standards.

Professional Society Leadership

As a Fellow of the North American Spine Society, Dr. Lee has demonstrated sustained contributions to the field of spine surgery. Fellowship status is granted only to members who have made significant contributions to spine care.

Long-Standing Board Certification

Board certified by the American Board of Orthopaedic Surgery since 2006, with certification valid through 2037. Dr. Lee has met rigorous examination standards and maintains ongoing continuing medical education requirements.
